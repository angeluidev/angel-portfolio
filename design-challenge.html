Design Challenge

Personal Project - April 2020

Being stuck at home during the Circuit Breaker period, I was looking for something to do while being stuck at home.

I came across the idea of doing design challenges and thought it would be a good way to flex my creative muscles. The theme was 'problems people face at home during the Circuit Breaker'. I interviewed my partner for the challenge.

User Need's Statement: Patrick is a single and young professional who's always keen to learn new things. He is looking for ways to fill small amounts of downtime in the day with an interesting activity in order to unwind but still acquire new knowledge.

Since I wanted to hone my wireframing skills in Sketch, I chose to build a digital product.

Solution: A mobile and web-app that allows you to listen to summarised versions of non-fiction books and articles - all in 15 minutes or less.
